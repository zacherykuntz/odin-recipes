Lasagne

Description

Mom's world famous lasagne, old world recipe straight from Italy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Box Lasagne Sheets
  • Ricotta
  • Basil
  • Tomato Sauce

Instructions

  1. Lay the pasta sheets out
  2. Put on the tomato sauce
  3. Layer some basil and ricotta
  4. repeat the steps untill all ingredients used
  5. bake for 40 minutes at 350 degrees
  6. eat that lasagne, king, you deserve it!
